About the event

A hackathon is an event where programmers, designers, and others collaborate on software or hardware projects within a short timeframe, like 24-48 hours. Teams innovate, solve challenges, and build solutions, fostering creativity and teamwork. Held by companies or universities, hackathons offer prizes, networking, and skill-building opportunities.
