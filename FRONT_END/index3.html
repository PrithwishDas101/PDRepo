Mythical creatures

dragon:
A dragon is a legendary creature appearing in folklore and mythology across various cultures. While depictions vary, dragons often feature wings, horns, and the ability to breathe fire, especially in Western traditions.
phoenix
The word "phoenix" can refer to a mythical bird or the city of Phoenix, Arizona. The mythological phoenix is a legendary, immortal bird that is reborn from its ashes, symbolizing rebirth and renewal.
vampire
A vampire is a mythical creature, often portrayed as undead, that preys on humans by consuming their blood. Vampires are a staple in folklore and fiction, particularly in European traditions, and are known for their fangs, bloodlust, and association with darkness and the supernatural.
werewolf
A werewolf, also known as a lycanthrope, is a fictional creature in folklore that can transform into a wolf or a wolf-human hybrid, often under the influence of a full moon.
